I'm Yssabel Pangilinan & Biologist Laboratory Specialist Student Programmer

Nice to meet you! Like the heading says, i'm Yssabel! I am the newest resident of San Jose, California. I was born and raised in sunny San Diego and graduated with my B.S. in Biology from San Diego State in 2015. Now I know what you're thinking... Biology? What is she doing in a coding class! I had the same exact thought too. I worked in Immunology biotech for the past 5 years and have now moved to a company that actually designs the best analysis software used for immunological data analysis. So cool! That is what peaked my interest in coding and why I am here today!
